Swimming is perfect exercise for your body.

Swimming is a fantastic low-impact exercise – you’ll end up feeling stronger, more flexible and less stressed, too. Plus, if you’d like to improve your technique, our expert swimming coaches are here to help, with a range of lessons and coaching on offer. Our swimming coaches are highly qualified who will make you better swimmer. Moreover, Active life club considers the health and safety of members as its first priority. Thus, the club has swimming guards who are ready for emergency situations and ensure health and safety in the outdoor and indoor swimming pools. Our members are in safe hands.

Feel refreshed after our sauna

Sauna is the best remedy for pain. As well as, it reduces stress after difficult day. Members are able stretch and relax their muscles in the sauna after training with gym. Using the sauna frequently makes people feel refreshed and improve their health as well. The club provides 2 saunas separate for male and female genders. The saunas work 7 days a week during working hours of the club.
