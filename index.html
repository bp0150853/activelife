FACILITIES

Whatever it is you need from us: the Active Life club offers wide variety of individual and group classes. It is friendly club and you will enjoy spending time with us. The club provides the world-class facilities. It is not just club where you train, it is club where you make lots of new friends. The club includes facilities of three sports such as fitness, tennis and swimming. This is ideal place to change your life to active life style and improve your health. Moreover, except from sports, the club members can find relaxing zone in the club where members can watch TV and have nice time with friends or family members. Sauna is provided as well where customers can relax after training.
